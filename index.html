About Us

Nadar Higher Secondary School was established in 1949 by the visionary leadership of Mr. S.S.T. Rathinasamy. Over the years, the school has evolved into a premier educational institution, offering quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our mission is to provide holistic development through academic excellence, co-curricular activities, and moral values.
